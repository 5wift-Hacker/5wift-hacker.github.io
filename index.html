Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Etsy Tracker app ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The Etsy Tracker is designed to work entirely on your device. All calculations you perform and any data you enter remain solely on your device and are never sent to us or any third party. We have no access to your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial data or calculations you enter
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Any other personal information

Data Storage

The Etsy Tracker does not store any data you enter. All calculations are performed in real-time and are not saved to your device or anywhere else. Once you close the app or navigate away, your entered data is cleared.

App Store Analytics

We use Apple's App Store Analytics to understand basic usage patterns such as app downloads, sessions, and crashes. This data is collected and managed entirely by Apple and is provided to us in an aggregated, anonymized format. We cannot identify individual users from this information. For more details on how Apple handles this data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Apart from Apple's App Store Analytics mentioned above, we do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Future Updates

We plan to add in-app purchase functionality in future updates. If this changes our data collection practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users accordingly before implementing any data collection.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
